Tortang Cabbage

Tortang Repolyo is a Filipino Cabbage Omelette made with cabbage, eggs, all purpose flour, chicken broth cube, pepper, salt and oil. This Cabbage Omelette recipe will show you how to cook Cabbage and egg in a delicious and scrumptious way. If you want a quick and easy meal. What's more is that it is packed with healthy goodness from the cabbage.

Preparation Time

  • Preparation: Approximately 5 minutes.
  • Cooking: Approximately 10 minutes.
  • Total: Approximately 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams (1/4 kilo) repolyo (cabbage)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¼ chicken broth cube (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

1.

Chop the cabbage into thin strips. After chopping, wash and drain well.

2.

In a mixing bowl, combine the cabbage strips, eggs and all-purpose flour. Dissolve the chicken broth cube in ½ tablespoon hot water. Add the broth mixture to the cabbage. Mix well.

3.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

4.

Heat a little bit of oil in pan. Scoop some cabbage mixture. Fry in low heat until light brown. Cover the pan while cooking.

5.

After a few minutes, flip the cabbage omelet to cook the other side. Cover again.

6.

When both sides are cooked, remove from heat. Fry the rest of the mixture.

7.

Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
